# Hook library (pattern families)

The recurring STRUCTURES that a corpus of ~1,000 documented viral hooks collapses
into, as clean fill-in templates. `text_hook` and `spoken_hook` writing pulls from
here. This file is generic by design: adapt every template into the creator's
voice from `radar-config.json` and make the specific detail theirs.

## How to use these (read first, it matters)

Never START from a hook. Start from a real reaction, a real premise, a real piece
of substance from the niche. Then two legitimate uses:
1. **Framing a true premise**: once the honest thing exists, use a family here to
   find the SHARPEST first-line phrasing of it (surface the buried lede).
2. **The burned on-screen hook text** (the ~6-word overlay, for mute viewers).

A copied hook with someone else's particulars rings false. The template is
scaffolding; the truth is the content. And per the storytelling craft rules in
`script-anatomy.md`: the hook must be a true TENSION, never a topic or a title.

## The families

**1. Transformation / before-after**
- This is your (X) before, during, and after (Y).
- From (before state) to (after state) in (#) steps.
- This is how (X) changed my life.
- Day (#) of going from (before state) to (after state).
- I got my (X) from this, to this.

**2. Exact-how / tutorial promise**
- Here's exactly how much (action/item) you need to (result).
- Everyone tells you to (action) but nobody tells you how. Here's a (#)-second tutorial you can save.
- In 60 seconds I'm going to teach you (result).
- Here's exactly what I'd do to (dream result).

**3. Time-credibility (effort or speed)**
- It took me 10 years to learn this. I'll teach it to you in under a minute.
- After a decade of (action), here's what I know.
- It took me (#) hours to (do thing) I thought would take (#) minutes.

**4. Targeted call-out ("if you...")**
- If you're (audience) and you want (result) by (avenue), listen to this.
- If you're between (age) and (age) and you feel (pain point), watch this.
- If you're in your (20s/30s/40s), here are the (#) things to do so you don't end up (pain) by (age).
- If you have (pain), (pain), and (pain) ... this is for you.

**5. Starting-over / hypothetical**
- If I were starting over in my (age) with no (resource), here are the top (#) things I'd do.
- If I woke up (pain point) tomorrow and wanted (dream result) by (time), here's exactly what I'd do.
- If I only had (time frame) to (result), here's what I'd focus on.

**6. Listicle**
- (#) things you need to do to (result).
- Top (#) ways to (action).
- 3 (people / channels / tools) that'll make you better at (skill) than a degree.
- (#) things I'll never regret doing.
- The 3 most common mistakes I see when people (action).
- (#) things you should never (action).

**7. Contrarian / myth-bust**
- Everyone tells you to (action), but here's why that's wrong.
- Stop (doing X) like this. Start doing it like this.
- Don't start (action) until you learn this.
- This is why (common action) is actually making you (pain point).

**8. Curiosity gap / cliffhanger**
- Did you know that this, this, this, and this get you (result)?
- Want to find out what's hiding inside (thing)?
- Apparently, if you (action) ... (don't resolve it in line one).
- This is kind of gross, but I had to share it.

**9. Comparison / this-vs-that**
- This is the same exact (thing), but the first got (result) and the second got (other result).
- This is what (X) looks like when you're (doing it right). And this is what it looks like when you're not.
- Ever wonder how the same (X) can give drastically different (results)?

**10. Story / personal**
- (#) years ago, (people) (did action). (Now ...)
- I (event) at (age), and it's what (changed everything).
- (#) years ago my friends and I started (thing). (Here's where it went.)
- This is (person). We've known each other since (when).

**11. Authority / earned flex**
- Not to flex, but I'm genuinely good at (skill).
- As a (title) for (#) years, here's what nobody tells you.
- I'm a (title), and this is the proper way to (action).

**12. Day-in-the-life / POV / confession**
- This is what my morning looks like while (situation).
- What I eat in a day on (X).
- Day (#) of (challenge / new routine).
- I'm (taboo or judgment-bait state), and this is my actual day.

**13. Interactive / challenge**
- Give me 10 seconds and I'll tell you (which X you are / if you have Y).
- In this video, see if you can tell what's real and what's not.
- Let's play "did you know," (niche) edition.

**14. Warning / stakes**
- If you're (age), do not do (action).
- If you don't have (X), do (Y) right now.
- If you want to end up (pain point), then skip this video.
- (#) things everyone should know how to fix in (situation).

**15. Result / proof reveal**
- This is what (#) of (item) actually looks like.
- This is how many (item) you really need to (result).
- I bought all the (X) so you don't have to. Here's what's worth it.

## Quick selection

- Personal story / behind-the-scenes moment -> families 1, 10, 12.
- Contrarian take in the niche -> 7, 9, 5.
- Building / authority / proof -> 1, 3, 11, 15.
- Taboo day-in-the-life VO (script-anatomy's @solidheaston engine) -> fuse 8 + 12:
  a confession-shaped state that fires judgment and curiosity at once.
- The on-screen ~6-word overlay -> compress any of the above to one claim.

Cross-use: this library is general. It works for LinkedIn openers, carousel
covers, and any short-form, not just TikTok.
