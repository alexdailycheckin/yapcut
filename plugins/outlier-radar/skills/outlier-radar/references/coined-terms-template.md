# Coined terms ledger (template)

Copy this file into your workspace as `coined-terms.md`. It is the
trend-creation pipeline for vocabulary (see "Trend creation" in SKILL.md and
section 5 of virality-psychology.md). A good candidate names a problem or
dynamic your audience already FEELS but has no word for. Rules: exact same term
every mention, one definition beat per week while in play, publish the term's
page on your site early (AI engines cite the coiner), graduate when a stranger
uses it unprompted, retire after ~4 weeks of no pickup.

Status values: `candidate` (in play, being planted), `graduated` (used by
others unprompted, keep using forever), `retired` (no pickup, dropped).
